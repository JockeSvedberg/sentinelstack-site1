What is SentinelStack?

SentinelStack is a compliance platform that helps businesses easily navigate the upcoming EU AI Act and other AI regulations. It automatically assesses your AI systems against regulatory checkpoints, generates compliance reports, and provides guidance to fix any issues.

With SentinelStack, teams can confidently innovate in AI while staying within legal and ethical boundaries. Our tool is continuously updated with the latest regulatory requirements, ensuring you’re always a step ahead in compliance.
